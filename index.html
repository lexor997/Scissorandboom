Recent Projects

Elevating Success and Redefining Possibilities

Restoring Architectural Splendor

Scissor and Boom was honored to be part of a significant restoration project involving a historical landmark. The aging structure required extensive refurbishment to preserve its architectural splendor for future generations. Our fleet of compact scissor lifts and articulating boom lifts proved instrumental in accessing delicate façade details and intricate stonework. Working in close collaboration with preservationists and skilled craftsmen, we carefully rejuvenated the landmark to its former glory, breathing new life into its unique heritage and inviting visitors to step back in time.

Elevating Fan Experience

A renowned sports arena embarked on a comprehensive renovation to enhance the fan experience and modernize its facilities. Scissor and Boom played a vital role in this project, providing an array of versatile equipment for different tasks. Our electric scissor lifts facilitated electrical and lighting upgrades, while our towable boom lifts allowed for seamless scoreboard installations and maintenance. With careful planning and adherence to strict safety protocols, we contributed to the arena's transformation into a cutting-edge sports and entertainment hub, delighting fans and athletes alike.

Boosting Manufacturing Capacities

A rapidly growing manufacturing company sought to expand its industrial complex to accommodate increased production demands. Scissor and Boom was enlisted to ensure smooth construction operations. Our fleet of rough-terrain scissor lifts and telescopic boom lifts were instrumental in erecting new structures and assisting with heavy machinery installations. With an emphasis on efficiency and project management, we helped the company scale up their operations, empowering them to meet market demands and drive innovation in their industry.
